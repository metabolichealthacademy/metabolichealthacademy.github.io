About Metabolic Health Academy

Metabolic Health Academy is dedicated to providing trustworthy, evidence-based educational information about metabolic health and chronic diseases associated with insulin resistance and cardiometabolic dysfunction.

Our mission is to empower individuals with understandable scientific information so they can make informed lifestyle choices and engage in meaningful discussions with their healthcare professionals.

We cover topics including:

  • Obesity and weight management
  • Insulin resistance
  • Prediabetes
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Hypertension
  • High cholesterol and lipid disorders
  • Metabolic syndrome
  • Fatty liver disease
  • Cardiovascular disease prevention
  • Nutrition and physical activity

Our Philosophy

We believe that high-quality health education should be accurate, understandable, and accessible to everyone. Our content is based on current scientific knowledge and is intended to support—not replace—the relationship between patients and their healthcare professionals.
